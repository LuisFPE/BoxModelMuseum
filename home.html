What is the Box Model Museum?

  We at Box Model Museum believe in art as a form of self-expression that transcends the limits of tradition. As such, we want to promote the use of innovative forms of art. We started playing around with Box Model during the pandemic, when everyone was particularly burnt out by the lack of social interaction, and quickly became interested in the idea of using Box Model to create our own works of art. It became clear to us that there was more to coding than working, and we decided to pursue that as a career. Three years later, we opened the Box Model Museum as a means to share this new experience with art enthusiasts and encourage them to create their own new forms of art.


How can I make my own Box Model Art?

  Box Model Art is a particularly easy and cheap way of making art: all you need is a working computer! It doesn't need to be the newest model or have the best specifications. As long as your computer works, you'll be able to use Box Model. The most basic program you might need is the Notepad that's already integrated in your computer. However, we recommend using an IDE or Integrated Development Environment.

  There are many different IDEs, but we recommend using Eclipse or Visual Studio Code. These programs can look a bit intimidating to those who aren't familiar with coding, but we assure you they are actually very easy to use!
